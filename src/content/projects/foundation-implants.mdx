## What it is

A complete redesign for a periodontics practice in Lone Tree, CO — 40 pages, premium animations, local SEO, integrated with Dentrix Ascend for appointment booking. Navy-and-gold palette, Cormorant Garamond display, glassmorphism cards, scroll-driven reveals, an interactive five-tab services panel, parallax hero, and a testimonials carousel that earns its glow.

## Why this is on a portfolio for AI roles

Two reasons. **One:** it proves I can carry an entire commercial project — discovery, design, build, content, deployment, ongoing relationship — on my own. **Two:** every page on it was a collaboration with an LLM. The brand voice doc, the FAQ structure, the Rank Math metadata, the JSON-LD schema, the patient-education long-form posts. AI as production tool, with a human carrying ultimate accountability for what gets published under a doctor's name.
