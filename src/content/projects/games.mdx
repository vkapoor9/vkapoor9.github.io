## What it is

A folder of interactive experiments — small games, physics toys, generative pieces. Most are throwaway; a couple keep getting opened months after they shipped.

I include them because the discipline of "is this actually fun to use" maps directly onto the discipline of "is this actually useful to a customer." The smaller the prototype, the more honestly the question gets answered.
