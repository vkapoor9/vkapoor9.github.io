## What it is

A simple, large-text expense tracker designed for one specific person: my mother. Indian rupee formatting, IST-aware date math, accounts (cash + bank), income tracking, donut and line charts, CSV export, soft-deletes. Installable as a home-screen icon.

Sometimes the most honest portfolio piece is the one nobody else will see: a working tool a real human relies on. This one earns its place.
